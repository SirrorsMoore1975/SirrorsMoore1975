## The Unhackable phone incident

On or about 2024 emergence the `unhackable phone` which notoriously known as the criminals goes to phone for criminal to avoid being tracked by the governemnt or the law enforcement. Forces in Belgium, France and the Netherland has infiltrated and decoded messages of the unhackable phone.

With the success in fighting organized crime via the infiltration, this sparks the public debates between personal privacy and security. New measures and laws are make to secure national security rather than protection of privacy. 

In some countries, such as France, has been actively framing projects such as GrapheneOS as aiding those organized crime, where all their methods, such as encryption, are legally obtained, none of their actions or provided features breaches any law, but with national security in mind and threaten to imprison developers, has make GrapheneOS to stop all their operation in France.
